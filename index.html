Tea

Preparation time: 10 minutes | Servings: 2

  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 tsp tea leaves
  • 1 tsp sugar (optional)
  • 1 tsp milk (optional)
  • 1 slice of lemon (optional)

Instructions

  • Boil 2 cups of water in a pot.
  • Add 1 tsp of tea leaves and simmer for 2-3 minutes.
  • Strain the tea into a cup.
  • Add sugar, milk, or lemon as per your preference.
  • Stir and serve hot.
